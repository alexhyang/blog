Life repeats itself over and over, but taking control of one's own life is never an easy thing. 
Every one of us was a born engineer to imagine and design the ideal life style and lives the 
hardest for this blue print. Methods are developed, just as those in scientific research, 
to solve the infinite cycles of problems. However, we are prone to drift along aimlessly, 
with all distractions in personal and professional life.
