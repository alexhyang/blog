A Life Tracker

Nov 24, 2022

Life repeats itself over and over, but taking control of one’s own life is never an easy thing. Every one of us was a born engineer. We imagine and design the ideal life style and live the hardest for this blue print. Methods are developed, just as those in scientific research, to solve endless cycles of problems. However, we are prone to drift along aimlessly, with all the distractions in our personal and professional life.
